My Hobbies

Badminton

Badminton is one of my favorite hobbies, and I regularly play it as a way to stay active and unwind. It’s a great way for me to improve my physical fitness, particularly my agility and coordination. I enjoy both singles and doubles matches, where I get to practice focus, uick reflexes, and teamwork, especially when playing with friends or classmates. Playing badminton helps me maintain a healthy balance between my academic commitments and personal well-being, offering a fun way to de-stress while staying in shape. It’s also a great social activity that strengthens friendships and promotes collaboration.
